Our Packages

From exploring lush tea plantations to experiencing thrilling wildlife safaris, our packages are designed to cater to every traveler's interests and preferences, ensuring an unforgettable journey through the enchanting landscapes of Wayanad.

CHEMBARA

The highest peak in Wayanad at an altitude of 6,890 ft above sea level. It is also one of the highest peaks in the western ghats that is above 2,000 m. The peak is visible from the different parts of Wayanad. It is located 8 km south of Kalpetta and near the town of Meppadi..

LAKKIDI VIEWPOINT

The Lakkidi viewpoint is a small viewpoint conducted just outside the main entrance gate of the Wayanad district on the main highway. The railings are high enough for safety, so you can get close to the edge. And just as a viewpoint, the views from here are truly beyond imagination..

SOOCHIPPARA FALLS

The waterfall cascades down the rocks from about 200 meters. A natural pool is formed below the falls, clear water asking you to dip in it. It's a mesmerizing and spellbinding sight. Surrounded by evergreen forests, Soochipara is among the best waterfalls in the region.

EN OORU

EN OORU,the first tribal heritage village in Kerala , introduces indigenous architecture coupled with tribal customs and traditions. Opened at Pookode in Wayanad district by the State Government, En Ooru is spread across 25 acres of land on the slope of a hillock..

EDAKKAL CAVES

Located at about 1200 ft above the sea level, the Edakkal Caves are among the oldest human settlements ever discovered. Inside the caves are pictorial drawings and engravings that indicate the presence of ancient human settlements in the region. Despite their name..

KARAPUZHA DAM

Karapuzha Dam located in the Wayanad district of Kerala, is one of the biggest earth dams in India. Karapuzha Dam is located in the greenish and natural regions of Wayanad, Kerala on the Karapuzha River, a tributary of the Kabini River. Construction on the dam began in 1977 and it was complete in 2004.

Book Now

BANASURA HILLS

Banasura Hill is one of the tallest mountains in the Western Ghats of the Wayanad district, Kerala, India. The hill is named after Banasura, a mythical character of Indian legends. It is one of the highest peaks exceeding 2,000m between Nilgiris and Himalayas after Chembra Peak.

Book Now

900 Kandi Glass Bridge

900 Kandi is a tourist destination located in Meppadi village of Wayanad district, Kerala. It is one of the hidden areas in the forest of Wayanad and are not visited by many tourists. The off-road trekking through the middle of the forest and a glass bridge located here are the major attraction at 900 Kandi..

Book Now

MUTHANGA WIDELIFE

Muthanga forest is the part of the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, with its entry point near Sultan Bathery. It borders Bandipur Reserve in Karnataka and the area is known for regular elephant sightings, which draws crowds for regular jeep safaris conducted by forest department..
